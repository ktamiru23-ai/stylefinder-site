Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

StyleFinder ("we", "us", or "our") operates the StyleFinder mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to operate the App:

  • Images you upload for visual search
  • Anonymous usage data such as number of searches performed
  • Device and diagnostic information (non-identifiable)

We do NOT collect:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment card numbers (handled by Apple)

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Process visual search requests
  • Provide search results
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Uploaded images are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently.

3. Camera and Photo Permissions

StyleFinder may request access to your camera and photo library only so you can upload images for visual search. Images are not used for advertising or marketing.

4. Analytics and Tracking

StyleFinder does not use third-party analytics tools that identify individual users. Any diagnostic data collected is anonymous.

5. Third-Party Services

StyleFinder may use third-party services to power search and image recognition. These services are required to comply with data protection standards.

6. Data Retention

Search images are deleted after processing. Anonymous usage data may be retained for service improvement.

7. Children's Privacy

StyleFinder is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any stored data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App means you accept changes.
